overview: >
  To accommodate a rebrand, I programmatically generated hundreds of blog post images from a design system I created. The system included four background variations and over 200 supporting graphics that could be paired with each background, resulting in more than 10,000 unique blog post image variations capable of covering nearly any blog post title. With this system in place, I could do almost anything: add new assets in Figma and seamlessly import them into the generator, access the generator from any web platform through embeds or webhooks, and programmatically create multiple images at once. The possibilities were endless.
